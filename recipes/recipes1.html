Description

Chicken and Rice Soup is a comforting and hearty dish, perfect for cozying up on a cold day. Made with tender pieces of chicken, nutritious vegetables, and a flavorful broth, this soup offers both warmth and nourishment. The rice, cooked directly in the broth, absorbs all the rich flavors, making each spoonful satisfying. It's the ideal meal when you're looking for something light yet filling, and its simplicity allows for endless variations. Whether you add a squeeze of lemon for a citrusy twist or extra herbs for depth, Chicken and Rice Soup is a timeless classic enjoyed by many.

The beauty of Chicken and Rice Soup lies in its versatility and ease of preparation. With basic ingredients such as carrots, celery, onions, and garlic, you can create a rich, aromatic base that infuses the soup with layers of flavor. The chicken adds protein and heartiness, while the rice provides a gentle texture that complements the broth perfectly. This soup is not only a comfort food but also a great way to use leftover chicken, making it an efficient and budget-friendly meal that everyone in the family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup white rice (uncooked)
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Saut√© vegetables:
    • In a large pot, heat butter or oil.
    • Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
  2. Add rice and broth:
    • Stir in the rice and chicken broth.
    • Bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer:
    • Reduce heat and let simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
  4. Add chicken and seasoning:
    • Stir in shredded chicken and dried thyme.
    • Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Finish and serve:
    • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
