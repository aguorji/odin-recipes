Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Home

Description

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup is a rich and velvety dish that blends the tangy sweetness of ripe tomatoes with the earthy freshness of basil. It's a comforting classic that brings warmth and flavor in every spoonful. Slow-cooked with onions and garlic, then blended with cream, this soup delivers a smooth, luxurious texture that’s perfect on its own or paired with a crusty grilled cheese sandwich. The basil adds a burst of herbal brightness that perfectly balances the rich creaminess of the soup.

This soup is not only delicious but also simple to make, using everyday ingredients like canned or fresh tomatoes, cream, broth, and a handful of spices. Whether served as a cozy dinner on a cold evening or as a light appetizer, Creamy Tomato Basil Soup is a crowd-pleaser. Its vibrant color and fragrant aroma make it both visually inviting and deeply satisfying—a dish that feels gourmet yet comes together easily in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Sauté the aromatics:
    • Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic; cook until softened.
  2. Add tomatoes and broth:
    • Pour in crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth.
    • Stir in sugar and bring to a simmer.
  3. Simmer the soup:
    • Let it simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes to blend flavors.
  4. Blend the soup (optional):
    • Use an immersion blender or a standard blender to puree the soup for a smooth texture.
  5. Add cream and basil:
    • Stir in heavy cream and chopped basil.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve hot with grilled cheese or crusty bread.
